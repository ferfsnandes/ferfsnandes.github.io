# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

The primary audience is commercial clients: small businesses, independent professionals, clinics, local service providers, and companies that need practical digital presence, better online lead capture, WhatsApp automation, or AI-assisted service flows. They usually arrive with overloaded support or sales routines, manual processes, weak digital presentation, or low confidence that their current site turns interest into qualified contact.

Secondary visitors may evaluate Gabriel Fernandes as a professional brand, but the site should primarily help a commercial buyer understand what Gabriel can solve, trust his execution, and start a conversation through WhatsApp or the contact form.

## Product Purpose

This site presents Gabriel Fernandes as a direct, reliable builder of digital solutions for businesses and professionals. It exists to convert visitors into commercial conversations while strengthening Gabriel's personal brand through clear offers, real work, technical credibility, and straightforward communication.

Success means a visitor can quickly understand the services offered, see enough proof to trust the work, and contact Gabriel with a project request through WhatsApp or the form.

## Brand Personality

Direct, reliable, and resolutive. The voice should feel practical, confident, and human: focused on the client problem, clear about outcomes, and free of exaggerated startup language.

## Anti-references

The site should not look like a generic AI-generated landing page, a cold corporate website, or an overly academic portfolio. Avoid template-like card grids, vague technology buzzwords, decorative AI tropes, and pages that feel made only to list coursework or isolated technical exercises.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the business problem before the technology.
- Make contact paths obvious without making the page feel pushy.
- Use real work and concrete outcomes as credibility signals.
- Keep the personal brand present, but let client value carry the page.
- Prefer concise, specific copy over generic claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain strong text contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation and forms, visible focus states, descriptive link labels, and motion that respects `prefers-reduced-motion`.
