## Writing standards
- Portuguese-native, problem-first (lead with the pain, then the fix).
- Each commercial page: 1 clear H1 (city + service), ≥600 words unique, `Service` + `BreadcrumbList` schema, WhatsApp CTA, ≥1 internal link to a case study.
- Each blog post: 1 H1, FAQ-style subheads (helps AI citation), 1 link to its service page.
- Never reuse a page by swapping only the city/service name (doorway-page risk).
